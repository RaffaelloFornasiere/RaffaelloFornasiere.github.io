About me

I have graduated in Electronic Engineering at the University of Udine, focusing my studies on Computer Science and Computer Networks. Now I am studying towards a master`s degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the Polytechnic of Milan. During my high school years I have come to love programming, and writing software has become my main field of interest ever since.

Education and history

Both the Technical Institute which I attended and the University have experienced me on how to work in groups as well as alone, and, at the same time, they taught me how to face different - and from time to time even unexpected - challenges. Furthermore, thanks to my undergraduate studies, I have developed skills in networking, software engineering, operating systems, optimisation and scheduling techniques. Among all of these, one of the most satisfying tasks that I worked on involved some combinatorial optimisation problems for my thesis work. There I got results above the expectations and, moreover, I got the opportunity to face and solve practical issues and this has been very stimulating for me. As a remark to my passion and eagerness to learn, during my spare time I’m used to develop personal projects that involve electronic and programming skills. This has definitely helped me gain experience, creativity, as well as the ability to continuously study and deal with new demanding tasks. I’m not afraid to work hard or to spend a lot of time learning something new, actually I would be excited to have challenging working possibilities and I am thirsty to become acquainted and experienced in new fields, in order to broaden my proficiencies.
